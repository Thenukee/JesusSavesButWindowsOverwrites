## Who I Am

Hello! I'm **Lean Beef Patty**, a researcher and student currently based in Sri Lanka. I'm pursuing my studies at the **General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University (KDU)**, exploring the fascinating intersections of peace & conflict studies, cybersecurity, and technology.

I believe that understanding conflict — from geopolitical tensions to cyber threats — requires both technical depth and human empathy. That's why I combine hands-on technical work (building tools, analyzing systems) with thoughtful writing and research.

## What I'm Interested In

- **Peace & Conflict Studies** — Understanding how conflicts emerge, evolve, and can be resolved, from regional disputes to information warfare.
- **Cybersecurity** — Digital forensics, network security, threat analysis, and building tools that protect systems and data.
- **Research & Writing** — Translating complex ideas into clear, accessible writing. Documenting what I learn and sharing it with others.
- **Technology for Good** — Using code and systems thinking to address real-world problems.
